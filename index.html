USDT to INR Taxation

Selling crypto from USDT to INR is considered a taxable event. When you convert USDT to INR, it's treated as a sale of cryptocurrency, and any gains made from this transaction are subject to capital gains tax.

Crypto Transfers

Transferring crypto between your own accounts (wallets or exchanges) is not a taxable event. However, you should maintain proper records of these transfers for tax reporting purposes.
